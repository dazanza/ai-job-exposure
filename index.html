Key Insights

Sources: ILOSTAT (2023), IMF Working Paper WP/25/43 (Cucio & Hennig, Feb 2025), Felten et al. (2021) AIOE Index, ILO (2026)
  • AI Exposure scores are derived from the Felten et al. (2021) AI Occupational Exposure (AIOE) index, mapped from SOC codes to ISCO-08 occupation groups.
  • Employment data sourced from ILOSTAT 2023, Philippines domestic Labour Force Survey.
  • Complementarity classification follows Pizzinelli et al. (2023) via the IMF framework, distinguishing roles where AI augments human work (high complementarity) from roles where AI substitutes tasks (low complementarity).
  • Sub-occupation employment estimates within ISCO major groups are approximations based on available disaggregated data and may not sum exactly to official ISCO group totals.
